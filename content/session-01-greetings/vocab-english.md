# Vocabulary — Session 1: Hello, Neighbor (English)

| # | English | Hebrew (עברית) | Arabic (العربية) |
|---|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Hello / Peace | שָׁלוֹם (Shalom) | مَرْحَبًا (Marhaba) |
| 2 | My name is... | ...שְׁמִי (Shmi...) | ...اِسْمِي (Ismi...) |
| 3 | Thank you | תּוֹדָה (Toda) | شُكْرًا (Shukran) |
| 4 | Please | בְּבַקָּשָׁה (Bevakasha) | مِنْ فَضْلِك (Min fadlak) |
| 5 | Yes | כֵּן (Ken) | نَعَم (Na'am) |
| 6 | No | לֹא (Lo) | لا (La) |
| 7 | Friend | חָבֵר (Chaver) | صَدِيق (Sadiq) |
| 8 | Where do you live? | ?אֵיפֹה אַתָּה גָּר (Eifo ata gar?) | أَيْنَ تَسْكُن؟ (Ayna taskun?) |
| 9 | Nice to meet you | נָעִים מְאוֹד (Na'im me'od) | تَشَرَّفْنَا (Tasharrafna) |
| 10 | Goodbye | לְהִתְרָאוֹת (Lehitra'ot) | مَعَ السَّلَامَة (Ma'a as-salama) |
