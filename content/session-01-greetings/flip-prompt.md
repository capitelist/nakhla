# Microsoft Flip Prompt — Session 1: Hello, Neighbor

## Topic Title
**Hello! Introduce Yourself**

## Prompt for Students

> Record a 30-second video: say your name, where you live, and one word in your language you want to teach your new friend.

## Instructions for Teacher

Post this prompt as the Topic description in Microsoft Flip:

```
🌴 Welcome to Nakhla!

Record a short video (30–60 seconds) where you:
1. Say your name
2. Tell us where you live
3. Teach us ONE word in your language — say it slowly so your new friends can learn it!

You can speak in any language — Hebrew, Arabic, or English. Be yourself!
```

## Settings
- **Video length:** 30–60 seconds
- **Moderation:** ON (teacher approves before visible)
- **Camera + screen recording:** Both enabled
- **Text responses:** Enabled (for students who prefer writing)

## When to Assign
- Assign in Google Classroom **2 days before Session 1** OR **after Session 1** as a follow-up
- Share the Topic link in both classrooms
- Encourage students to watch and reply to each other's videos
