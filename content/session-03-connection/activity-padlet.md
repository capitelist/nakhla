# Activity: Friendship Postcard — Collaborative Padlet

## Overview
Each pod creates a digital "postcard" post on Padlet celebrating their new friendship.

**Duration:** 15 minutes (within pod pair block, last 15 minutes)
**Group:** Pod pairs posting to shared Padlet board
**Tool:** Padlet (padlet.com) — no student account needed

---

## Setup (Teacher, Before Session)
1. Create a Padlet board: **"Nakhla — Friendship Postcards"** (Wall layout)
2. Set to "Can write" for anyone with link
3. Enable "Require approval" so teacher reviews posts before they appear
4. Create a sample post showing the format:

### Template Post Format
Each pod creates ONE post together:
```
🌴 FRIENDSHIP POSTCARD 🌴
FROM: [Pod Name / All 4 Names]

SOMETHING FROM OUR CULTURE:
🇮🇱 [Israeli kids describe or draw something from their culture]
🇦🇪 [Emirati kids describe or draw something from their culture]

✉️ OUR MESSAGE:
In English: ________________________
In Hebrew: _________________________
In Arabic: _________________________

Signed: [all 4 names]
```

---

## Instructions for Kids

**EN:**
1. **Draw/Describe:** Israeli kids: share something from YOUR culture. Emirati kids: share something from YOUR culture. Show each other your worlds!
2. **Write:** Together, write a friendship message in ALL 3 languages. Help each other with the words!
3. Sign all 4 of your names
4. Be creative — add images, drawings, or voice recordings!

**עב:**
1. **ציירו/תארו:** ילדים ישראלים: שתפו משהו מהתרבות שלכם. ילדים אמירתיים: שתפו משהו מהתרבות שלכם. הראו אחד לשני את העולמות שלכם!
2. **כתבו:** ביחד, כתבו הודעת חברות ב-3 שפות. עזרו אחד לשני עם המילים!
3. חתמו את כל 4 השמות שלכם
4. היו יצירתיים — הוסיפו תמונות, ציורים, או הקלטות קול!

**عر:**
1. **ارسموا/صِفوا:** الأطفال الإسرائيليون: شاركوا شيئاً من ثقافتكم. الأطفال الإماراتيون: شاركوا شيئاً من ثقافتكم. أروا بعضكم عوالمكم!
2. **اكتبوا:** معاً، اكتبوا رسالة صداقة بالـ٣ لغات. ساعدوا بعضكم بالكلمات!
3. وقّعوا بأسمائكم الأربعة
4. كونوا مبدعين — أضيفوا صوراً، رسومات، أو تسجيلات صوتية!

---

## Sharing (In Full Group Activity Block)
- Teacher screen-shares the Padlet board with the full group
- Each pod reads their trilingual message aloud
- Teacher saves the board link — this IS the Session 3 portfolio
- Share the Padlet link with parents alongside the friendship certificate
