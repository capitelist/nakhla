# Blooket Setup — Session 3: Friends Across the Shore!

Step-by-step to create the Blooket game from `kahoot-questions.json`.

---

## Create the Question Set

1. Go to **blooket.com** and log in to your teacher account
2. Click **Create** → **Create a Set**
3. **Title:** "Nakhla Session 3: Friends Across the Shore!"
4. **Description:** "Final review quiz — everything we learned across all 3 sessions"
5. Add each question from `kahoot-questions.json`:

### Questions to Add

| # | Question | Correct Answer |
|---|----------|---------------|
| 1 | How do you say 'Friendship' in Hebrew? | חֲבֵרוּת (Chaverut) |
| 2 | How do you say 'Friendship' in Arabic? | صَدَاقَة (Sadaqa) |
| 3 | Review! How do you say 'Thank you' in Hebrew? | תּוֹדָה (Toda) |
| 4 | Review! How do you say 'Delicious' in Arabic? | لَذِيذ (Ladhidh) |
| 5 | What does 'Yachad' (יַחַד) mean? | Together |
| 6 | What does 'Hadiyya' (هَدِيَّة) mean? | Gift |
| 7 | 'Shalom' and 'Salama' both come from a root meaning... | Peace |
| 8 | What grows in BOTH Israel and the UAE? | Date palms |
| 9 | How do you say 'Let's stay friends' in Hebrew? | בּוֹא נִשָּׁאֵר חֲבֵרִים (Bo nisha'er chaverim) |
| 10 | What's the most important thing we learned in Nakhla? | We have more in common than we think! |

6. For each question: enter the question text, add 4 answer options, and select the correct one
7. **Time limit:** 30 seconds per question
8. Click **Save**

---

## Host the Game

1. From your Blooket dashboard, find the Question Set
2. Click **Host**
3. Choose game mode: **Gold Quest** (recommended for the final session — celebratory feel)
4. Students go to **play.blooket.com** and enter the Game ID
5. Students enter a nickname (first name only) — no account needed
6. Share your screen via Zoom so everyone sees the game
7. This is the final quiz — make it a celebration! Read answers in all 3 languages, cheer every correct answer

---

## Tips
- This quiz reviews ALL 3 sessions — it's a victory lap
- The last question ("What's the most important thing we learned?") is the emotional capstone — let it land
- After the quiz, transition to the Friendship Postcard activity on Padlet
- Free tier: unlimited games, up to 60 players
