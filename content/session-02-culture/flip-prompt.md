# Microsoft Flip Prompt — Session 2: Our World

## Topic Title
**My World — Food, Family, Traditions**

## Prompt for Students

> Show us your favourite food or a family tradition. Explain it in your language — we will translate!

## Instructions for Teacher

Post this prompt as the Topic description in Microsoft Flip:

```
🌴 Nakhla Session 2: Our World

Record a short video (30–60 seconds) where you:
1. Show or describe your FAVOURITE FOOD or a family tradition
2. Explain it in YOUR language — Hebrew, Arabic, or English
3. Don't worry about translating — your friends will help!

Ideas: show us a dish, a photo, a game you play, a song, or something your family does together. Be creative!
```

## Settings
- **Video length:** 30–60 seconds
- **Moderation:** ON (teacher approves before visible)
- **Camera + screen recording:** Both enabled
- **Text responses:** Enabled (for students who prefer writing)

## When to Assign
- Assign in Google Classroom **after Session 2** as a follow-up activity
- Encourage students to reply to their pen pal's video with their own video response
- Teacher can feature standout videos at the start of Session 3
